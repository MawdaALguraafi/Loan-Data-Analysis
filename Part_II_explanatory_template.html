Investigation Overview

his project analyzes a dataset from Prosper, a peer-to-peer lending platform, which contains detailed records of over 113,000 loans issued between 2005 and 2014. Each entry in the dataset represents a unique loan and includes information about borrower profiles (such as employment status and income range), loan characteristics (including loan amount, interest rates, and loan terms), and loan outcomes (such as current, defaulted, or completed status).

The primary goal of this exploratory data analysis is to understand how different borrower and loan attributes influence borrowing costs and loan performance. By examining key variables and their relationships, we aim to uncover patterns that can help in assessing loan risk and borrower creditworthiness. Visualizations will be used to support insights and guide further analysis
